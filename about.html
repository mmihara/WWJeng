About Us

Wood Wiley and Jebian Consulting Structural Engineering is a privately held professional corporation which has provided quality structural engineering services to clients throughout California since 1959. Located in Fresno, we are ideally situated to serve the diverse needs of the steadily growing economies of the Central Valley and Central Coast areas.

Our firm provides a wide range of structural consulting services from new construction to projects requiring building modernization, alteration, or damage repair.

Over the years our firm has maintained a reputation for providing unparalleled service in the design of Medical, Educational, Municipal, Industrial, and Commercial projects.

Years of experience have made us the primary Central Valley providers of structural engineering services for the seismic anchorage and support of Medical and Imaging equipment, as well as HVAC and Electrical equipment, ducts, conduits, and fire sprinklers.

We regularly work with the Building Agencies for the Cities and Counties of Fresno and Madera, the Division of the State Architect (DSA) and the Office of Statewide Health Planning and Development (OSHPD). Our firm’s extensive experience has allowed us to develop strong working relationships with key members of these agencies both in Sacramento and locally. This close relationship enables us to serve our clients efficiently when dealing with these agencies.

The staff at Wood Wiley and Jebian are committed to providing quality service and meeting the needs and demands of our clients. Our focus is to provide the best support possible to ensure the success of the project and our client’s satisfaction.

History

1946 - Arthur A. Sauer began providing Consulting Structural Engineering services to the Sacramento and Stockton area following his service as Chief Structural Engineer with the Los Angeles County Architect, Engineer in Charge of the Structural Section of the California Division of the State Architect, and Assistant Supervisor Civil Engineer in the C.E.C. Corps and Naval Construction Regiment, Japan, for the U.S. Navy.

Mr. Sauer served on many technical committees that provided advisory services on seismic design for structures and formulated many of the policies enforced today by Division of the State Architect and OSHPD, which review the design of Schools and Hospitals in California.

The company in the early years was known as Sauer-Marr-Grimes-Wood Consulting Engineers, with Arthur Sauer & Joe Wood in the Sacramento office, and Kenneth Marr & Charles Grimes in the Stockton office.

1959 - Service was expanded in the Central Valley with Joe Wood starting the Fresno office marking the beginning of our company’s service to the Fresno area.

1971 - The firm becomes Sauer-Wood & Associates, with Arthur Sauer in the Sacramento office and Joe Wood & Vance Wiley in the Fresno office.

1979 – Upon Mr. Sauer’s retirement from the firm, Joe Wood partnered with Vance Wiley in the Fresno office, forming Wood-Wiley & Associates.

1995 - Joe Wood retires from the firm, making Vance Wiley the President of the company. Joe Wood continued to consult part time until 1998.

2006 - After working with the company for 23 years Anthony Jebian became the new owner following the retirement of Vance Wiley. The firm was incorporated under the name Wood Wiley & Jebian. Vance Wiley continues to consult with the company part time as projects require.
